MOESI has 34 transitions across 5 states and 4 cores. The state space is too large
for random simulation to give you any confidence. Formal property checking is not
optional.

## Invariants, not properties

The temptation is to write SVA properties for every transition. Don't. Write the
four global invariants: single-writer, no-stale-shared, no-deadlock,
forward-progress. Properties follow.

The cleanest piece of advice I received as a junior engineer was: every nanosecond
you spend in a flop is a nanosecond you cannot spend in logic. At 7nm, that
nanosecond is worth roughly four gates. Treat it accordingly.

## Spend the budget early

Architectural pipelining decisions are 100x cheaper than retiming after
place-and-route. If a path looks long in RTL, it is long in silicon — there is no
margin to win back later.
